Jamaican Rice and Peas

Jamaican Rice and Peas are a staple in any Jamaican household to pair with almost any meat dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice, washed and drained
  • 1 can (14oz or 400ml) Red Kidney beans.
  • 1 cup Fresh coconut milk
  • 1 small onion, chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed.
  • 2 stalk green onion.
  • 2 sprig of thyme
  • 1 Scotch Bonnet pepper, Do not cut or burst
  • 5 Pimento berries (allspice)
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp black pepper
  • 1 2/3 cups water

Directions

  1. Drain beans into measuring cup. Add coconut milk and add enough water to make 4 cups
  2. Add the liquid, beans, whole stalks of green onion, pimento, black pepper, and salt to a large pot. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add rice, and bring to a boil.
  4. Turn heat to low and cook covered until liquid absorbed (20-25 min.)
  5. Fluff with a fork. Remove green onion, pepper, and thyme before serving.
